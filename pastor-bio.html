Meet Our Leaders

Dedicated servants called by God to lead, teach, and shepherd our congregation with love and faith.

Pastor & First Lady

Pastor James Williams

Senior Pastor & Founder

Pastor James has served the Rochester community for over 20 years. He founded New Hope Community Church in 2004 with 12 members meeting in a living room. Today the church serves hundreds of families.

His ministry focuses on building strong families, empowering youth, and creating a welcoming space for everyone to experience Christ's transformative love. He holds a Master of Divinity.

First Lady Michelle Williams

Co-Pastor & Women's Ministry Director

First Lady Michelle leads the Women's Ministry, Marriage Enrichment programs, and the church's food pantry serving 200+ families monthly.

Her "Sisters in Faith" women's group is a cornerstone of spiritual growth for women throughout Rochester. Michelle's warmth and wisdom have touched countless lives.

Our Story

New Hope Community Church was born from a prayer and a vision. In 2004, Pastor James and First Lady Michelle felt called to plant a church that would be a beacon of hope in Rochester, NY.

What started as a small Bible study in their home has grown into a thriving community of faith committed to authentic worship, relevant teaching, genuine community, and compassionate outreach.
