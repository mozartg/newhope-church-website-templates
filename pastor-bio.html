Pastor & First Lady

The heart and vision behind New Hope Community Church

Pastor James Williams

Senior Pastor & Founder

Pastor James has been serving the Rochester community for over 20 years. With a heart for people and a passion for God's Word, he founded New Hope Community Church in 2004 with just 12 members meeting in a living room. Today, the church serves hundreds of families across the region.

His ministry focuses on building strong families, empowering youth, and creating a welcoming space where everyone can experience the transformative love of Christ. Pastor James holds a Master of Divinity and is known for his dynamic preaching and genuine care for the community.

First Lady Michelle Williams

Co-Pastor & Women's Ministry Director

First Lady Michelle is the backbone of New Hope's community outreach. With her background in counseling and her gift of hospitality, she leads the Women's Ministry, Marriage Enrichment programs, and the church's food pantry that serves over 200 families monthly.

Her "Sisters in Faith" women's group has become a cornerstone of spiritual growth for women throughout Rochester. Michelle's warmth and wisdom have touched countless lives, and she continues to be a shining example of grace and servant leadership.

Our Story

New Hope Community Church was born from a prayer and a vision. In 2004, Pastor James and First Lady Michelle felt called by God to plant a church that would be a beacon of hope in Rochester, NY. What started as a small Bible study in their home has grown into a thriving community of faith.

Through every season, the church has remained committed to its founding principles: authentic worship, relevant teaching, genuine community, and compassionate outreach. Whether you're taking your first steps in faith or looking for a church home, you'll find family here at New Hope.
